FitnessCalculatorAir

Introduction

FitnessCalculatorAir is a web-based application for quick and easy calculation of key health and fitness metrics. Users can determine their body mass index (BMI), body fat percentage, ideal weight and other values - directly in the browser and without installation. In the planned next expansion stage, the project can be expanded to include storage functions, visualizations and integration into modern frameworks such as Angular.

Description

Body-Mass-Index

 Weight + Height The BMI (body mass index) is a measure that indicates the ratio of body weight to height and is often used to assess underweight or overweight. It is calculated by dividing the body weight in kilograms by the square of the height in meters

Body-Fat-Percentage

 Height + Neck + Waist The body fat percentage indicates what proportion of body weight consists of fat mass. It is an important indicator of health, as too high or too low a percentage is associated with an increased risk of various diseases

Ideal-Weight

 Height Ideal weight is a term that generally refers to a person's optimal weight range for health, based on various factors such as height, stature, age and gender. It is often defined as the weight at which life expectancy is highest or mortality is lowest

Basal-Metabolic-Rate

 Weight + Height + Age + Gender Basal metabolic rate (BMR) refers to the minimum amount of energy your body needs to function at rest, including maintaining essential processes like breathing, circulation, and cell function. It's a measure of how many calories your body burns to sustain these basic functions
